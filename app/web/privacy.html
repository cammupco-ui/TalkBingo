Privacy Policy for TalkBingo

Last updated: February 03, 2026

TalkBingo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data on external servers without your explicit consent. Minimal user data (nickname, game preferences) may be stored locally on your device or in our secure database for authentication purposes.

2. Microphone Usage (Record Audio)

Our app requires access to your device's microphone ("RECORD_AUDIO" permission) solely for the generic Speech-to-Text (Voice Recognition) feature during gameplay.

  • Audio data is processed by your device's operating system (Android/iOS) to convert speech into text.
  • We do not record, save, or transmit your voice data to our servers.
  • This permission is used strictly to enable voice inputs for the Bingo game.

3. Data Retention

If you choose to delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our database.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@talkbingo.app
