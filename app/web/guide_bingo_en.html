TalkBingo Game Rules

TalkBingo is not just Bingo. The core is to gain "Sympathy (Approve)" from your opponent through conversation.

1. Bingo Cell Types

T Type Truth Game

  • Answer the given question honestly (Text/Voice).
  • Opponent must "Approve" your answer to claim the cell.
  • Reward: Claim Cell + EP +1

B Type Balance Game

  • Choose one of two options and explain your reason.
  • Opponent must accept your reason and "Approve" to claim the cell.
  • Reward: Claim Cell + EP +1

M Type Mini Game (Revival)

  • If a quiz fails, the cell becomes Locked.
  • Anyone can tap a locked cell to trigger a Mini Game.
  • The winner of the mini game claims the cell immediately!
2. Winning Conditions
  • Complete 1 Bingo Line first and declare [Finish Game] to win!
  • Select Continue to challenge for higher rewards.
  • Draw: If both complete Bingo simultaneously or the board is full without a winner.
