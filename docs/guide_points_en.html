Points Guide

VP

Victory Point (VP)

The most prestigious point earned by the Game Winner.

• Earn: Win +50 VP (2x/3x for Streaks)
• Use: Remove Ads (Cost 200 VP)
AP

Activity Point (AP)

Earned whenever you complete a Bingo line.

• Earn: 1 Line (+20), 2 Lines (+40), 3 Lines (+60)
• Convert: 100 AP → 50 VP
EP

Experience Point (EP)

Earned by passing quizzes or claiming cells.

• Earn: Claim Cell +1 EP
• Convert: 100 EP → 50 VP
