Privacy Policy

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

TalkBingo processes personal information for the following purposes. processed personal information will not be used for purposes other than the following.

  • Member registration and management
  • Game record storage and recovery
  • Service provision and improvement

2. Items of Personal Information Processed

The Company processes the following personal information items:

  • Required: Email address, Nickname, Date of Birth, Gender

3. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

In principle, users' personal information is destroyed without delay once the purpose of collecting and using personal information involves achieved. However, it may be destroyed immediately upon membership withdrawal or stored for a certain period in accordance with relevant laws.

4. Destruction of Personal Information

Members can destroy their personal information and terminate the use contract at any time through the 'Settings > Delete Account' menu.
